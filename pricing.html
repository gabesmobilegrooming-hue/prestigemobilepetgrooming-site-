Pricing & Mobile Spa Services

Services and package pricing for dogs and cats. Final quotes are based on coat condition, size, and behavior.

Core Spa Services

Every full-service appointment includes a customized mix of the services below, adjusted for each dog or cat.

  • Hydro-massage bath – Warm water circulates around the body to deep clean the coat and help support healthy skin and blood flow.
  • Specialty shampoo – A variety of professional shampoos chosen for your pet’s specific coat and skin needs.
  • Specialty conditioner & de-shedding treatment – For double-coated breeds we include a premium de-shedding treatment to help dramatically reduce loose undercoat.
  • Hand dry & blow dry – Hand dryers only; we do not use cage dryers. Coats are dried fully to help prevent matting and keep your pet comfortable.
  • Thorough brushing – Removes tangles, loose hair, and surface debris while stimulating circulation and promoting a healthy coat.
  • Ear cleaning – Gently cleans the ears to help reduce buildup that can lead to irritation.
  • Nail trim – Nails are cut to an appropriate length to keep your pet walking comfortably.
  • Nail grind (where suitable) – Nails are smoothed with a grinder to round any sharp edges.
  • Foot & pad trim – Excess hair on and around the feet is trimmed for a tidy, safe finish.
  • Paw pad shave – Helps keep pads clean and reduces slipping on smooth floors.
  • Nose & paw balm – Moisturizing balm is applied to help soothe and protect delicate skin on the nose and paw pads.
  • Teeth brushing – Light cosmetic brushing to help freshen breath and support oral care between vet cleanings.
  • Body trim / haircut – Coat is trimmed or styled as requested for comfort, maintenance, or a specific look (breed or custom style).
