Pricing & Mobile Spa Services

Luxury Mobile Grooming for Dogs & Cats

Pricing below is a guide for our full-service packages. Final quotes are based on your pet’s size, coat type, condition, behavior, and grooming history.

Core Spa Services

Every full-service appointment includes a custom mix of the services listed below, adjusted for each dog or cat.

  • Hydro-massage bath – Warm water circulates around the body to deep clean the coat and support healthy skin.
  • Specialty shampoo – Premium shampoos chosen for your pet’s specific skin and coat needs.
  • Specialty conditioner & de-shedding treatment – For double-coated breeds we use a premium de-shedding product to help reduce loose undercoat.
  • Hand & blow dry – Hand dryers only; we do not use cage dryers.
  • Thorough brushing – Helps remove tangles, loose hair, and surface debris while stimulating circulation.
  • Ear cleaning – Gently cleans the ears to reduce buildup that may lead to irritation.
  • Nail trim & nail grind – Nails are cut and, when appropriate, smoothed with a grinder for a softer edge.
  • Foot, pad & sanitary trim – Excess hair is trimmed for a clean, tidy finish.
  • Paw pad shave & nose/paw balm – Helps protect and moisturize sensitive areas.
  • Teeth brushing – Light cosmetic brushing to freshen breath between veterinary cleanings.
  • Body trim / haircut – Coat is trimmed or styled as requested for comfort, maintenance or a breed-style look.
